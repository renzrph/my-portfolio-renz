Websites I have made.

Nutrition Facts Calculator

Created a user-friendly calculator using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to streamline the computation of nutritional information for product labeling. The calculator saves time and effort for regulatory pharmacists handling nutritional products by computing kcal from protein, non-protein calorie, carbohydrate RENI, fat RENI, and % RENI per serving. Designed to be efficient and accurate, the calculator provides a reliable tool for nutrition labeling.
